FAQ

How do I install the app on my device?  

You can download Rock Paper Tuition from both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Simply search for our app name, tap install, and you'll be ready to start learning through games in just a couple minutes. The app requires about 200MB of storage space.

How does the game-based learning work?  

Our system transforms traditional school subjects into engaging game experiences. Each lesson becomes a level to complete, where students solve problems to earn points and rewards. As they progress, they unlock new challenges that align perfectly with their curriculum, making learning feel more like playing their favorite game while mastering important concepts.

Can parents monitor their child's progress?  

Parents receive full access to a dedicated dashboard that tracks all learning activity. This includes detailed reports on which subjects their child has studied, time spent learning, achievements earned, and areas that may need more focus. We also send weekly email summaries to keep parents informed about their child's educational journey.

Is my progress saved if I switch devices?  

Yes, all your learning progress is securely saved to your account in the cloud. Whether you switch from an Android phone to an iPad or use multiple devices, simply log in with your account credentials and you'll pick up right where you left off. We recommend connecting to Wi-Fi when switching devices for seamless synchronization.

Can I install Rock Paper Tuition on my phone?  

Rock Paper Tuition works on most modern smartphones, including iPhones (iOS 13+) and Android devices (8.0 Oreo or newer). If you can download it from your app store, your device is compatible. For best performance, we recommend phones with at least 2GB RAM and 250MB storage space. Still unsure? Just try installing it.

How do I contact customer support?  

Our support team is available by email at info@rextechstudios.com for any questions or issues you may encounter. We pride ourselves on quick response times, typically replying within one business day. Whether you need technical assistance, have billing questions, or want feature explanations, our friendly support staff is ready to help.
